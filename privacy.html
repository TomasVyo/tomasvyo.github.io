PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated July 10, 2026

Tomáš Vyorálek ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Notice describes how we collect, use, and share information through the PubMate mobile application.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email, and username. For features like BAC calculation, body weight and gender are required but are stored strictly locally on your device and never sent to our servers.

Social & Location Features

When you enable social features, we collect and process data to facilitate friend requests, leaderboard rankings, and location sharing. Your precise location is used to help you find pubs and, if enabled, shared only with users you have explicitly accepted as friends.

2. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES & ANALYTICS

We utilize third-party services to enhance the App:

  • Supabase: Used for secure database hosting and user authentication.
  • Google AdMob: Collects device identifiers and usage data to serve advertisements.
  • Google Play Billing: Manages in-app purchases. We have no access to your payment card details.

3. DATA SHARING & DISCLOSURE

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with third-party service providers as necessary to perform functions such as database management, payment processing, or ad delivery. We may also disclose information to comply with legal obligations.

4. DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

5. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as the right to access, correct, or delete your data. You can delete your account and all associated data directly in the app via Settings -> Profile -> Delete account and data, or by emailing us at tomasvyo.dev@gmail.com.

6. UPDATES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be indicated by an updated "Last updated" date.
