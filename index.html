PubMate

The ultimate mobile beer counter for your night out.

How it works

PubMate is a minimalist app designed to quickly track your beer consumption without distractions. Create your personal list of pubs, start a session, and count your drinks with a single tap. We don't use GPS, ensuring your privacy and saving your battery life.

Why we need your data

PubMate offers Google Sign-In to securely create your account, back up your local database, and sync your drinking stats and favorite pubs across your devices. We only request basic profile information strictly for authentication purposes.

We are currently preparing for our official launch on the Google Play Store. Stay tuned!
